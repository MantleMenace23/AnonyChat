About This Project

Hi! This is a personal project I built as a student in the district. My goal was to create something safe, clean, and useful for my friends and classmates.

  • Chat: A private chat tool that makes it easier to collaborate on school projects, especially for students without phones or who find email slow.
  • Games: A small collection of simple games meant purely for entertainment at home. There’s a disclaimer at the top of the games page reminding students not to use them during school hours.

This project is mainly about learning, coding, and creating something helpful. The games are just an extra feature for fun outside of school.
